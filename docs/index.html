How It Works

Three steps.
One verdict.

No spreadsheets. No manual calculations. No guessing. PARSIO does the heavy lifting — you make the call.

01
📄

Upload the Statement

PDF or Excel. Encrypted or plain. PARSIO detects the bank, the layout, and the format — automatically. No template setup required.

02
⚡

Fraud Protection

Recycling & Blacklist Detection

PARSIO flags round-trip transfers, repeated credits designed to inflate turnover, and duplicate applications across sessions. Blacklist management built in.

Risk Engine

DTI, Turnover & Rate — computed

Debt-to-income ratios, applicable turnover (NTB trimmed average, renewal 3-month recency window), location-adjusted interest rates. All calculated automatically.

Reporting

PDF Reports & Excel Exports

Every decision generates a professional PDF report — ready to attach to a file, share with a borrower, or archive for audit. Full Excel breakdowns available on demand.

Audit & Analytics

Full Admin Dashboard

Session tracking, officer activity, bank distribution, approval rates, monthly trends — everything a credit manager needs to run a clean operation.
